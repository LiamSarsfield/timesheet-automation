# NAS Branding Restyle — Shape

## Brand Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| `nas-green` | `#006B3F` | Primary: header, buttons, selected states |
| `nas-green-dark` | `#004F2D` | Hover states |
| `nas-green-light` | `#E8F5EE` | Hover backgrounds (calendar, re-download) |
| `nas-green-50` | `#F0FAF4` | Light button backgrounds |

## Header
- Dark green bar (`bg-nas-green`) with `shadow-md`
- NAS crest logo (`/nas-crest.png`) at `h-12`, left-aligned
- White title + subtitle (`text-white`, `text-white/70`)
- Flex layout: logo + text side by side

## Employee Details Card
- Green top stripe: `border-t-[3px] border-t-nas-green`
- Section heading in NAS green

## Buttons
- Submit: NAS green with dark hover
- Re-download: Light NAS green background, NAS green text/border
- Download modal buttons: NAS green with dark hover

## Monday Picker
- Selected Monday: `bg-nas-green text-white`
- Hoverable Monday: `hover:bg-nas-green-light`

## Favicon
- NAS crest logo as favicon
